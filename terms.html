Terms of Use

Last updated: April 2026

By using the Airports Transfer Turkey website and booking our services, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully before placing a booking.

1. Services

Airports Transfer Turkey provides private airport transfer services in Istanbul and surrounding areas. All transfers are carried out by licensed, professional drivers using well-maintained vehicles.

2. Booking & Confirmation

A booking is confirmed once you receive a written confirmation from us via email or WhatsApp. Prices are fixed at the time of booking and will not change unless you modify the route or number of passengers after confirmation.

3. Payment

  • Payment can be made in cash (EUR, USD, GBP, TRY) or by card on the day of travel.
  • No prepayment is required unless agreed otherwise.
  • Prices include all taxes, tolls, and standard waiting time.

4. Cancellation Policy

  • Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time.
  • Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee.
  • No-shows without prior notice will be charged the full amount.

To cancel or modify a booking, please contact us via WhatsApp or email as soon as possible.

5. Flight Monitoring & Waiting Time

We monitor all incoming flights in real time. Standard complimentary waiting time is:

  • 60 minutes after the actual landing time for international flights
  • 30 minutes after the actual landing time for domestic flights

Extended waiting beyond these periods may incur an additional charge.

6. Passenger Responsibilities

  • Passengers must provide accurate pickup details, flight numbers, and contact information at the time of booking.
  • The number of passengers and luggage must not exceed the capacity of the selected vehicle.
  • Passengers are responsible for their personal belongings throughout the journey.

7. Limitation of Liability

Airports Transfer Turkey is not liable for delays caused by extraordinary traffic conditions, road closures, adverse weather, or other circumstances beyond our reasonable control. We always strive to deliver punctual and reliable service.

8. Amendments

We reserve the right to update these Terms of Use at any time. The latest version will always be available on our website. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of any revised terms.
