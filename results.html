2.1 Placing a Booking

Bookings can be placed through the Airports Transfer Turkey website, via WhatsApp at +90 544 102 1414, or by email at bekpenturizm@gmail.com. When placing a booking, you must provide accurate and complete information including:

The mere completion of an online booking form does not constitute acceptance until a written confirmation has been issued by us.

Airports Transfer Turkey requires a minimum of 12 hours notice before the scheduled pickup time. Bookings placed with less than 12 hours notice may be accepted at our discretion but cannot be guaranteed.

3. Pricing and Payment

3.1 Fixed Pricing

All prices quoted by Airports Transfer Turkey are fixed at the time of booking and will not change regardless of traffic conditions, route diversions, or flight delays. There are no hidden fees, surcharges, or additional charges unless you request services beyond what was originally booked.

3.2 Currency

Prices are displayed in Euros (EUR) on the Platform. Airports Transfer Turkey also accepts payment in US Dollars (USD), British Pounds (GBP), and Turkish Lira (TRY). The exchange rate applied for non-EUR payments will be the rate current at the time of the transfer.

Payment is collected directly by the driver upon completion of the transfer. Airports Transfer Turkey accepts:

Extra charges may apply in the following circumstances, provided they are communicated and agreed upon before or during the transfer:

  • Additional stops not included in the original booking

  • Change of destination after the transfer has commenced
  • Additional passengers or luggage exceeding the booked capacity, requiring a larger vehicle

4. Cancellations and Modifications

4.1 Free Cancellation

Airports Transfer Turkey offers free cancellation for any booking cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time. No cancellation fee, penalty, or charge of any kind will be applied. To cancel a booking, simply contact us via WhatsApp or email with your booking reference.

4.2 Late Cancellation

Cancellations made less than 24 hours but more than 6 hours before the scheduled pickup time may be subject to a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the agreed transfer price. Cancellations made less than 6 hours before the scheduled pickup time, or failure to cancel altogether (no-show), may result in the full transfer price being charged.

4.3 Modifications

Changes to an existing booking (date, time, pickup location, destination, number of passengers) can be made free of charge up to 12 hours before the scheduled pickup time, subject to vehicle and driver availability. Modification requests should be sent via WhatsApp or email. If the modification requires a different vehicle category, the price difference (if any) will apply.

4.4 Cancellation by Airports Transfer Turkey

In rare and exceptional circumstances (severe weather, natural disasters, government-imposed travel restrictions, or other force majeure events), Airports Transfer Turkey may cancel a confirmed booking. In such cases, we will notify you as soon as possible and offer either a full rebooking at no extra cost or a complete refund of any amounts already paid.

5. Flight Monitoring and Pickup Procedure

5.1 Real-Time Flight Tracking

For all airport pickups, Airports Transfer Turkey monitors your flight status in real time using live flight tracking technology. If your flight arrives early or is delayed, your driver arrival time will be adjusted automatically. You do not need to inform us of flight delays as our system handles this for you at no additional charge.

5.2 Meet and Greet

Your Airports Transfer Turkey driver will meet you inside the airport terminal at the arrivals hall. The driver will hold a name board displaying your name as provided during booking. Prior to your arrival, you will receive the driver name, photograph, and direct mobile phone number so you can contact them if needed.

5.3 Pickup from Other Locations

For non-airport pickups (hotels, residences, cruise ports, etc.), your driver will arrive at the agreed address at the scheduled time. The driver will contact you by phone or WhatsApp upon arrival. It is your responsibility to be ready at the pickup point at the agreed time.

6. Waiting Time Policy

6.1 Complimentary Waiting Time

Airports Transfer Turkey provides the following complimentary (free) waiting time from the scheduled pickup time or actual flight landing time:

  • Airport pickups (domestic flights): 45 minutes from the actual landing time
  • Airport pickups (international flights): 60 minutes from the actual landing time
  • Hotel / address pickups: 15 minutes from the scheduled pickup time
  • Cruise port pickups: 60 minutes from the vessel docking time

6.2 Extended Waiting Time

If you require additional waiting time beyond the complimentary period, please inform your driver. Extended waiting time is charged at a rate that will be communicated by the driver at the time of the request. The charge is applied per 15-minute increment.

6.3 No-Show

If the Traveller does not appear at the pickup point within 30 minutes after the complimentary waiting period has expired, and cannot be reached by phone, the transfer will be recorded as a no-show. In the case of a no-show, the full transfer price may be charged. Airports Transfer Turkey will make at least three attempts to contact the Traveller by phone and/or WhatsApp before marking a transfer as a no-show.

7. Luggage and Passenger Policy

7.1 Luggage Allowance

Each vehicle category has a maximum luggage capacity as displayed at the time of booking. Standard luggage is defined as a suitcase weighing up to 23 kg with dimensions not exceeding 80 x 50 x 30 cm. Small personal items such as handbags, backpacks, and laptop bags are not counted toward the luggage allowance.

7.2 Excess Luggage

If you arrive at the pickup point with more luggage than was declared in the booking and the assigned vehicle cannot safely accommodate it, Airports Transfer Turkey may arrange an additional vehicle at an extra cost. If an additional vehicle is not available, Airports Transfer Turkey shall not be responsible for any items left behind.

7.3 Special Items

Items such as oversized sports equipment (surfboards, skis, bicycles), musical instruments, or bulky items must be declared at the time of booking. Failure to declare such items may result in the driver being unable to accommodate them.

7.4 Child Seats

Child seats and booster seats are available upon request at no additional charge. Please indicate the need for a child seat (including the child age and weight) at the time of booking so we can ensure the correct equipment is provided.

7.5 Passenger Capacity

The number of passengers must not exceed the capacity stated for the booked vehicle category. For safety and legal reasons, the driver has the right to refuse transport if the actual number of passengers exceeds the booked amount and cannot be safely accommodated.
