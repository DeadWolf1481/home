Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Airports Transfer Turkey ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

When you make a booking or contact us, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Pickup and drop-off locations
  • Flight number and travel date
  • Number of passengers and luggage
  • Payment information (processed securely — we do not store card details)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal data solely to:

  • Process and confirm your transfer booking
  • Coordinate your pickup with your assigned driver
  • Monitor your flight and adjust pickup times if needed
  • Send booking confirmations and important travel updates
  • Respond to customer support enquiries

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing

Your information may be shared only with:

  • Your assigned driver (name and pickup details only)
  • Our internal operations team to manage the booking
  • Payment processors, solely to complete the transaction

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil your booking and comply with applicable legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

6. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies to ensure basic functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.
